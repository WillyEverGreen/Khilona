History

The Legacy of Indian Toymaking

India’s toy-making tradition spans thousands of years, with roots in ancient civilizations and local artistry. Crafted from natural materials like wood, clay, and cloth, these toys represent regional culture, myths, and everyday life. They were designed not only for play but to teach values and tell stories, making them a cherished part of Indian heritage. At Khilona, we celebrate this legacy, bringing traditional toys that connect generations and preserve the artistry of India’s past.
Craftsmanship

The Art of Craftsmanship

Every toy in our collection is a piece of art, handmade by skilled artisans who use traditional techniques passed down through generations. From hand-carved woodwork to intricate hand-painted designs, each toy reflects a dedication to quality and heritage. At Khilona, we honor the artistry and dedication of India’s craftsmen and women, whose hands breathe life into every toy.
